Case Study:

Walkwithautism.com

Walk with Autism is a small nonprofit organization aimed at improving community awareness, and providing resources to those that need them.

Visit Site

Research:

  • Through user research, we identified the site's 3 key purposes:
  • Give current information on events and activities
  • Gather donations
  • Encourage volunteers, vendors, and sponsors to sign up

Problem / Ideation:

The current website has no focused purpose, issues with intuitive navigation, and the overall look and feel does not instill confidence.

Iteration:

  • After some testing, it was determined these changes would be necessary to further improve the site:
  • Modernize the site's design
  • Highlight Donations/Contributions
  • Clearly state the organization's goals
  • Condense and remove redundant information that was previously listed in multiple areas of the site
